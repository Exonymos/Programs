Tasty Bites is India's No.1 Restaurant.

HEALTHY FOOD IS A BASIC NEED & NOT LUXURY

Tasty Bites is the 1st, Indian fast food Quick and Home Service Restaurant that primarily sells Home Made Tasty Food items from Whole Wheat, Salads & Tray Meals. It is fastest growing single brand that serves best quality food items in fast food style. It is owned and operated by two college friends.

The concept behind the birth of Tasty Bites is to serve the clients with healthy, tasty & nutritious eco-meal prepared from Whole Wheat. Each Food Items Express are hand tossed with love & garnished with our secret spices.

We provide recyclable plates and edible spoons for saving our environment's future.

Within few years Tasty Bites will changed the definition of delicious healthy food.
